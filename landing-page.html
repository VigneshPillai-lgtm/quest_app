Frequently Asked Questions

What is Skyfall?

+

Skyfall is a 48-hour hackathon where 1000+ teen students from across India come together to build innovative projects, learn new skills, and connect with industry leaders.

Who can participate?

+

Students aged 13-19 from anywhere in India are eligible to apply. All skill levels welcome!

Is there a cost?

+

No! It's completely free. We cover food, accommodation, and all resources you need.

Never hacked before?

+

Perfect! We have mentors and workshops for all skill levels. Everyone belongs here.
