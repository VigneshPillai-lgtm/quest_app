Frequently Asked Questions

What is Skyfall?

+

Skyfall is a 48-hour hackathon where 1000+ teen students from across India come together to build innovative projects, learn new skills, and connect with industry leaders.

Who can participate?

+

Students of any education level from anywhere in the world are eligible to apply. All attendees must be 13 years or older.

Is there a cost to attend?

+

No! Admission is completely free. We cover food, accommodation, and all resources you need to build an amazing project.

What if I've never hacked before?

+

Perfect! Skyfall welcomes teen hackers of all skill levels. We provide workshops, mentorship, and resources to help you succeed.

When and where is Skyfall?

+

Skyfall takes place in September at a central location in India. Applications open in June. Follow our updates for exact dates and location!
