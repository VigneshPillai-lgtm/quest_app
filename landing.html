Questions?

FAQs

Everything you need to know before the big day.

Who can participate in Skyfall?

Any teen student at the campus can join! No prior experience or skills needed — Skyfall is designed to be fun and accessible for everyone.

Do I need to install anything?

Nope! Skyfall runs entirely in your browser. Just open the app, sign in with your email, and you're ready to go. Make sure you have camera access enabled.

Can I participate solo or in a team?

Skyfall is currently designed as a solo quest — each participant has their own profile and tracks their own progress. But feel free to explore with friends!

What happens if my camera doesn't work?

If you experience camera issues, please notify one of the event organisers. We'll have admin tools available to help you check in manually at each venue.

How is the winner determined?

Winners are ranked by total XP earned. You gain XP by visiting venues and completing their missions. The more venues you complete, the higher your score!
